I joined Youpi team in early 2021 as the founding designer. I am leading design projects on designing expressive & interactive camera experience and immersive social video experience. My responsibility includes high-level strategy design, user research & interview, market analysis, feature delivery, branding, UX design, UI design, design library setup, devs & QA's support, company website & landing page design etc. I collaborate with product & eng team on the end-to-end design of the app.
