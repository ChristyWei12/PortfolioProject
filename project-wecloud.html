What is WeCloud?

WeCloud is a secured online platform where you can save your files and share with others. It aims to bring a fun and pleasant experience for busy professions, especially in tech, and students in college and university.

Problem

Nowadays the cloud storage and organization product is becoming more and more popular, however, most of the existing products are too complicated - users often find it hard to organize the files due to complexity of the structure, or lacking fun to use - the UI is looking too serious and formal.

Solution

WeCloud wants to blow a new fresh air into this field providing a more colorful, relaxing and fun user experience that users can upload files, save files and collaborate with others especially under the current trend of working and collaborating remotely.

Discovery

User Survey

User survey is helping me better understand on how people think about the cloud management market and what is their real desire. Surveys are sent out via Google Forms. Keys questions are listed as below:

What cloud management product do they use?
What features do they use most?
What are the deterrents that make them look for another app/website?
How do they like to share the file?
Are they using these kind of product for personal or business use?

Here are some key insights from survey:

  • 89%
    of attendees have experience using cloud content management website/app
  • 96%
    of attendees use the website/app to upload files to the cloud
  • 76%
    of attendees will stop using the product if there is no free space or very limited space provided
  • 64%
    of attendees would like to share the files via link

Personas

User survey provides a good basis for creating the user personas. 2 distinct groups are identified among survey attendees:

the student group who are studying in university or graduate school and have the need to save files and share with others for academic projects;

the business professionals who are working in a company and have needs to process work files and also collaborate with others at work.

Bio

Susan is a college student in UK. Her major is international affairs. This semester, she has a few team projects to work on.

Motivation

To better conduct team work to finish the course requirement, Susan would like to find an online software that enables her to upload files as well as share with other team members. Cost is also a big concern for her.

Frustration

it is costly for additional space charge; slow upload speed

“A product that is easy to use as well as cheap is important to me.”

Steve Wang

Steve is a product manager living in Cupertino, California. His daily work includes managing different projects and collaborating with other teams.

Steve cares very much about the efficiency and security of the product. He is looking for a product that is easier to use, has high upload speed and high level of security.

Comparative Analysis

The competition in the cloud content management market is very fierce and many big brands are stepping into this field. I did a competitive Analysis of Google Drive, Dropbox and Egnyte, which are the big players in the market and got insights in Visual Design, User Flow, Brand & Logo design etc.

Identity

Branding is a big challenge for me. It is very important to give the product a special name that is attractive and easy to remember, and at the same time expresses the meaning that can help people organize their files and connect with others. Here is the mind map for brainstorming.

Among all the concepts above, I decided to choose WeCloud as the brand name. “Cloud” indicates the cloud space where files can be saved and shared and “We” indicates the collaboration between people.

Typography

Montserrsat is chosen as the logo font and Open Sans as the UI and text font. Both typefaces belong to sans serif type family. Montserrat using as logo font delivers the brand characteristic as being modern, clean and pleasant. Open Sans is selected to reflect WeCloud as a tool for work and study.
