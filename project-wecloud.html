What is WeCloud?

WeCloud is a secured online platform where you can save your files and share with others. It aims to bring a fun and pleasant experience for busy professions, especially in tech, and students in college and university.

Problem

Nowadays the cloud storage and organization product is becoming more and more popular, however, most of the existing products are too complicated - users often find it hard to organize the files due to complexity of the structure, or lacking fun to use - the UI is looking too serious and formal.

Solution

WeCloud wants to blow a new fresh air into this field providing a more colorful, relaxing and fun user experience that users can upload files, save files and collaborate with others especially under the current trend of working and collaborating remotely.

Discovery

User Survey

User survey is helping me better understand on how people think about the cloud management market and what is their real desire. Surveys are sent out via Google Forms. Keys questions are listed as below:

What cloud management product do they use?
What features do they use most?
What are the deterrents that make them look for another app/website?
How do they like to share the file?
Are they using these kind of product for personal or business use?

Here are some key insights from survey:

  • 89%
    of attendees have experience using cloud content management website/app
  • 96%
    of attendees use the website/app to upload files to the cloud
  • 76%
    of attendees will stop using the product if there is no free space or very limited space provided
  • 64%
    of attendees would like to share the files via link
