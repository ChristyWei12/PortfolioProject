Who am I?

Hey, I am Christy Wei, a UX designer based in the San Francisco bay area, California. I believe in the power of design which can make our life easier and more pleasurable.

Why I love UX Design?

Sometimes there is no reason why you are passionate about something. You just love it from the first sight, you enjoy it, and you want to spend countless hours on it and you will feel achievement and satisfaction. Sometimes it feels like it is inherent in your blood. For me, this thing is UX design.

A little about my background

Before I found UX design, I have been trying to explore different possibilities from the every angle of my life to find the thing that I am really passionate about: I learned dancing for 4 years that enabled me to enjoy the charm of music; I was an illustrator intern for a local newspaper while in school and I started to find my passion on sketching and paintings; I like traveling to explore various lifestyles, make new friends and document it through my camera; I majored in Philosophy at university which makes me form the habit of thinking and tend to dig deeper into people’s inner feelings and thoughts and stay empathetic; studying data analytics during graduate school and working as an audit specialist helped me build the analytical skills and forced me to make conclusions that need to be supported by data. Then I joined the Smart Closet Team where I learned UX Design.

Startup Experiences: Smart Closet

Smart Closet is a virtual closet mobile platform where people can create outfit looks, shop new items and share their fashion collage with others. As the co-founder, I am responsible for marketing, PR, user research and customer relationship management. In my daily work, I spent a lot of time talking to our users, collecting their feedback and putting insights into the design improvement. I am not a UX designer for Smart Closet though, but I have the opportunity to work in user research and work together with the engineering team to make the user experience much easier and full of fun. The more I got involved in optimizing the design, the more I enjoyed it. The way that we can make a difference in people's life -- make their life easier and more pleasurable -- gives me a sense of achievement. From this experience, UX design stepped into my life and successfully attracted my attention.

A new journey starts now

I know making a career change requires a big commitment so I joined the Bloc UX Design Apprentice Program to systematically study UX Design. After months of studying and working on projects, I was able to combine my passion for design with the analytical skills I gained in the past to become a creative problem solver. From learning UX fundamentals, to developing the visual skills, to learning front-end development skills, e.g. HTML, CSS, Javascript etc, I am happy to see myself gradually grow to be a professional designer. I am excited to start a new journey as a UX designer to make people’s life easier and more pleasurable.
