A new social camera

Social apps are meant to connect people with others, however, people are feeling less connected with others for various reasons: on trending apps like TikTok and Instagram, top performers have much higher chance to gain viewersâ€™ attention and leave accounts with fewer followers hard to get their stories seen; Giving likes are dominating how people interact with others and deeper interactions are needed; Once connected as friends online, people find it hard to push their relationship a step further etc. Nowadays people are seeking new ways to better connect and interact with others thus building positive relationships with people in their community.

Bubble Camera seeks to offer a solution helping people connect with others genuinely and interact with others deeply. It is our 2nd product exploring the possibilities of camera apps. It aims to create a new social camera that connects people via disappearing video threads and various remix tools. Our team believes that serendipity among people can be built in a fun and creative way.
