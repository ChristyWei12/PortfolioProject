SMART CLOSET

Manage Your Closet & Share Your Style

What is Smart Closet?

Smart Closet is a clean, smart and easy-to-use tool to help you manage your wardrobe and daily outfit.

Smart Closet has achieved over 1 million downloads 🍻 in App Store and Google Play. It is ranked as 🎖 #1 in Shopping in the App Store with a review of 4.5/5 stars.

User Survey & Interview

User surveys are conducted on a regular basis to collect the feedback and comments from Smart Closet users. User Interview is also conducted to keep our team connected with users and keep empathy.

Work With Engineering Team

Intensive user research provides a solid support for product improvement and new feature launching. After putting insights into the design process, I worked together with the engineering team to launch new features, including launching social feed functions, adding favorite collections etc.

Build & Manage Fashion Community

In order to keep a positive communication with Smart Closet users, I built fashion communities in social media e.g. Instagram, Facebook etc. and built the blog at WordPress.

To keep our communities active and fun, I designed and organized weekly fashion contests that encourage users to post outfit looks under certain themes.

Marketing

I conducted multiple marketing campaigns at platforms including Facebook, Instagram etc. Influencer Marketing drives great growth and collaborating with influencers explores new potential markets and builds authentic content.

Tutorial

I created marketing content including tutorials to demo new feature launched.
